Keywords: AI and jobs, job displacement, AI and hiring, automation, female-dominated fields
Implications: The increasing automation driven by AI is causing concerns about job displacement, particularly in sectors traditionally dominated by women. This necessitates proactive measures to address potential societal and economic inequalities.

Keywords: Adobe Creative Cloud, AI features, subscription costs, price increases
Implications: The incorporation of AI features into creative software is leading to higher subscription costs, raising concerns about accessibility and affordability for artists and professionals. This could lead to shifts in software usage patterns and the exploration of alternative tools.

Keywords: AI energy consumption, data centers, environmental impact, sustainability
