2. Climate Migration and Refugee Crisis

Keywords: Climate refugees, climate migration, Bangladesh, India, flooding, sea level rise
Implications: Climate change is driving mass migration and creating a refugee crisis, particularly in vulnerable coastal regions. This puts immense strain on receiving countries and necessitates international cooperation to address the root causes and manage displacement.

Keywords: Wildfire smoke, asthma, respiratory health, eye health, heat waves
Implications: Climate change poses significant threats to public health through increased exposure to wildfire smoke, extreme heat, and the spread of infectious diseases. This necessitates proactive public health measures and improved healthcare infrastructure to mitigate the effects.
