Keywords: Supply chain security, critical mineral access, rare earth supply chains, strategic manufacturing, economic diversification
Implications: The trade war is highlighting the vulnerabilities of global supply chains. Nations are increasingly focused on diversifying their sources of critical minerals and components to reduce reliance on single suppliers and enhance resilience against disruptions. This will likely lead to reshoring and nearshoring of manufacturing.
