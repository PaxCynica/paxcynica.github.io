Keywords: insurance, climate risk, carbon markets, gas prices, economic hardship
Implications: Climate change is increasingly impacting financial markets and the global economy. Insurance companies are facing rising costs, and businesses are adapting to climate-related risks. Energy sector instability and price volatility are also major concerns.

Keywords: climate apathy, public safety, Billie Eilish, environmental networks

Keywords: food scarcity, drought, hunger, WFP, permaculture
