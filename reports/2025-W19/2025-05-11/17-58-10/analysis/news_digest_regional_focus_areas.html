Keywords: Gaza, humanitarian aid, Israel, blockade, starvation, children, hospitals, food shortage

Keywords: US tariffs, China, trade war, global economy, consumption, Thailand, economic impact
Implications: The ongoing trade tensions between the US and China, marked by high tariffs, are negatively impacting global economic growth. Decreased consumer spending in China and uncertainty in export-oriented sectors in countries like Thailand highlight the far-reaching consequences of these trade disputes. This instability risks triggering a wider global recession.
