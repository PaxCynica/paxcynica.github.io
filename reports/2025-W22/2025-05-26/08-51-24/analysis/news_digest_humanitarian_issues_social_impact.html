Analyst’s Guidance

  • Closely monitor the situation in Gaza, paying particular attention to reports of civilian casualties and humanitarian aid access. Al Jazeera's reporting provides a valuable perspective.
  • Assess the evolving international response to the conflict and the potential for escalation. The statements of world leaders and international organizations are crucial data points.
  • Analyze the role of misinformation and propaganda in shaping public opinion and fueling the conflict. Understanding the sources and spread of these narratives is critical.
  • Evaluate the legal implications of alleged war crimes and genocide, and the potential for international justice mechanisms to be activated. Monitor developments from the International Criminal Court (ICC).
  • Identify opportunities for humanitarian assistance and support for victims of the conflict. The needs are vast and require coordinated international efforts.
