Keywords: US, China, France, Emmanuel Macron, spheres of coercion, Southeast Asia, East Asia, alliances

This could lead to new strategic partnerships and shifts in global power dynamics.

Keywords: Israel, internal unrest, protests, military service refusal, Netanyahu government, public opinion
Implications: The Gaza conflict is exacerbating existing divisions within Israeli society. Growing protests and refusal of military service signal deep discontent with the government's handling of the situation.
