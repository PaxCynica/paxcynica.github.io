Keywords: Climate change, Trump administration, Inflation Reduction Act, state-level policy, federal policy
Implications: The divergence between federal and state climate policies creates uncertainty and hinders national-level progress. Keywords: Climate change, global warming, extreme weather, climate myths, scientific consensus

3. Climate Change Impacts on Indigenous Communities

Keywords: Climate change, Indigenous communities, deforestation, Amazon, UNPFII, climate refugees
Implications: Climate change disproportionately affects vulnerable populations, particularly Indigenous communities. The destruction of their traditional lands and livelihoods poses significant social, economic, and cultural risks. This necessitates targeted policies and support to ensure their resilience and participation in climate solutions.
