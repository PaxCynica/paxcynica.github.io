Keywords: nuclear proliferation, Iran nuclear program, ballistic missile capabilities
Implications: Israel and other nations express strong concerns about Iran's nuclear program and ballistic missile capabilities. President Donald Trump's stated intention to attack Iran if a nuclear deal is not reached underscores the gravity of the situation.
