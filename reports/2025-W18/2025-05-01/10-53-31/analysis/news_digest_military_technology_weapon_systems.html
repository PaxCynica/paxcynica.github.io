Keywords: drone strike, counter-drone, FPV Drone, Shahed Drone, robotic warfare, Lumberjack drone
Implications: The increasing use of drones in military conflicts highlights the evolving nature of warfare. The effectiveness of deck guns against drones in close-range engagements, as seen in the Red Sea, suggests a potential countermeasure.
