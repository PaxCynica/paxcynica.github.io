3. Disinformation and Election Interference

Keywords: disinformation campaigns, information warfare, election interference, digital authoritarianism, election monitoring
Implications: The spread of disinformation poses a serious threat to democratic processes worldwide. The use of social media and other digital platforms to manipulate public opinion and interfere in elections is becoming increasingly sophisticated. Combating this requires international cooperation and innovative strategies to identify and counter false narratives.

Keywords: election, voter suppression, election monitoring, democratic transition, political polarization
