Keywords: clean energy, renewable energy, fossil fuels, China, Europe, US, energy transition, geopolitical tensions
Implications: The global shift towards renewable energy sources is uneven, with some nations like China rapidly expanding renewables while others, like the US under the Trump administration, are reverting to fossil fuels. This uneven transition creates geopolitical complexities, impacting international relations and the effectiveness of global climate action.
