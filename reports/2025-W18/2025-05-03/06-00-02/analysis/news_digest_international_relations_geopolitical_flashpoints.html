1. Trump's Impact on Global Affairs

Keywords: Trump, tariffs, sanctions, Iran, Ukraine, Russia, global order
Implications: President Trump's policies are significantly altering the global order established after WWII, leading to increased trade tensions, uncertainty in international relations, and potential economic instability. His aggressive use of tariffs and sanctions against China and Iran is causing widespread economic repercussions. His approach to the Ukraine war and the Iran nuclear deal is creating further instability.
