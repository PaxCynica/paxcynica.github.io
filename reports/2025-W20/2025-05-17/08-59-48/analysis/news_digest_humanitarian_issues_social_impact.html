Keywords: Gaza, Israel, famine, humanitarian aid, blockade, war crimes, genocide, child malnutrition, acute food insecurity
Implications: The ongoing conflict in Gaza has created a severe humanitarian crisis, with widespread food shortages and a high risk of famine. The blockade imposed by Israel severely restricts access to essential supplies, exacerbating the situation. International organizations are struggling to provide adequate aid due to funding cuts and logistical challenges. The situation raises significant concerns about human rights violations and potential war crimes.

Keywords: ICC, Donald Trump, Karim Khan, sanctions, war crimes, genocide, justice, international law
