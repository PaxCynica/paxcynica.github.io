Keywords: China, Belt and Road Initiative, resource nationalism, strategic investments, Latin America
Implications: China's increasing economic influence, particularly through initiatives like the Belt and Road Initiative, is reshaping global trade and partnerships. This includes significant investment in Latin America as the US reduces its aid. This shift in global power dynamics presents both opportunities and risks for various nations.
