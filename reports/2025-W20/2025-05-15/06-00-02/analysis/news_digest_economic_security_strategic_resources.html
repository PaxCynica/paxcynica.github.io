Keywords: Belt and Road, China, Colombia, Latin America, infrastructure, strategic investments

Keywords: tariffs, trade sanctions, oil prices, semiconductor industry, agriculture, automotive industry

Keywords: Gaza, food security, Israel, blockade, humanitarian crisis, sanctions policy
