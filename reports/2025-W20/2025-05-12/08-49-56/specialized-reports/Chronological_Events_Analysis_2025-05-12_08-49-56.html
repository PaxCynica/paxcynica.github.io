Summary

May 12, 2025, witnessed a confluence of significant events impacting global stability and humanitarian concerns. Simultaneously, peace negotiations between Russia and Ukraine, and India and Pakistan, offer fragile hopes for de-escalation, while the US plays a complex mediating role.
