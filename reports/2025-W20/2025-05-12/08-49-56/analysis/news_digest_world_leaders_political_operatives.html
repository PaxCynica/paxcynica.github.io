Keywords: Russia, Ukraine, Putin, Zelenskyy, ceasefire, peace talks, sanctions, Istanbul

Keywords: Putin, Ukraine, ceasefire, sanctions, 30-day truce, European leaders, Donald Trump

The threat of further sanctions adds weight to the demand, potentially influencing Putin's decision-making. The success or failure of this ultimatum will significantly impact the trajectory of the conflict and the broader geopolitical landscape.
