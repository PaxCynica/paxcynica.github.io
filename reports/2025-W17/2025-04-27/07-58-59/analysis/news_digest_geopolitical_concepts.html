Keywords: Gaza, Israel, blockade, humanitarian aid, food shortage, famine, ceasefire violations

Keywords: Russia, Ukraine, peace talks, Donald Trump, Vladimir Putin, Steve Witkoff, sanctions, Crimea, annexation

Keywords: Iran, US, nuclear negotiations, sanctions, Oman, uranium enrichment, diplomatic negotiations
