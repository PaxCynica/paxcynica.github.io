  • Pay close attention to official announcements from the US State Department and the Syrian government regarding any changes in US policy towards Syria. Analyze the potential impact on regional stability and the fight against ISIS.

  • Be vigilant about monitoring political developments in countries experiencing democratic backsliding. Look for signs of government overreach, restrictions on civil liberties, and attempts to undermine democratic institutions.
  • Stay informed about new sanctions and trade policies implemented by major economic powers. Analyze the potential impact on global markets and supply chains.
