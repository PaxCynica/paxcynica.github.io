2. Rare Earths & Critical Minerals Security

Keywords: Rare Earths, Critical Minerals, China, US, Supply Chain, National Security, Ukraine
Implications: China's dominance in rare earth minerals is prompting the US and other nations to seek alternative sources and bolster domestic production. This includes exploring partnerships with countries like Ukraine and Greenland. Restrictions on rare earth exports could impact various industries, from defense to healthcare, and accelerate the onshoring of critical industries.
