US sanctions are hindering Nvidia's business in China, creating opportunities for Huawei. Additionally, Boeing is facing challenges as Chinese airlines reject aircraft, leading to potential shifts in orders for companies like Malaysia Airlines.

Implications: Escalating trade tensions could reshape the global tech landscape, impacting market access and supply chains. The aviation industry faces rising costs and potential order shifts. Companies need to diversify their markets and supply chains to mitigate risks.
